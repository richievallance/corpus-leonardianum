## Abstract

The *Corpus Leonardianum Universale* is a unified scholarly ecosystem reconstructing the complete mechanical, philosophical, and scientific thought of Leonardo da Vinci from primary codex sources. This repository serves as the master navigation hub and infrastructure portal, cross-linking all constituent repositories under the governing title *Grammatica Naturae, Lex Motus, et Ars Vivens* (The Grammar of Nature, the Law of Motion, and Living Art). The ecosystem operates on a three-layer reading model: Text = demonstration; Reverse sequence = instruction; Margins = governing laws.
